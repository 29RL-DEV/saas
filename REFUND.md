## 1. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

We stand behind our Service. If you're not satisfied, we offer a 14-day refund guarantee under the following conditions:

### 1.1 Refund Window

- **Duration:** 14 calendar days from date of purchase
- **Starts:** When you complete your first payment
- **Ends:** At 11:59 PM on day 14

### 1.2 Usage Limits

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

- Have used less than **20%** of the Pro features
- Not have received a refund previously on your account
- Provide a valid reason for the refund

**Feature Usage Examples:**

| Scenario                       | Eligible | Reason                |
| ------------------------------ | -------- | --------------------- |
| Subscribed but never logged in | ✅ Yes   | Didn't use features   |
| Used 2-3 Pro features          | ✅ Yes   | Within 20% usage      |
| Used 8+ Pro features           | ❌ No    | Exceeded 20% usage    |
| Subscribed 30 days ago         | ❌ No    | Outside 14-day window |

## 3. REFUND PROCESSING

### 3.1 Refund Timeline

```
Day 0:  Request submitted
Day 1:  Eligibility review
Day 2:  Approval/denial notification
Day 7:  Refund processed (average)
Day 10: Funds appear in account (varies by bank)
```

**Note:** Bank processing times vary. Check with your bank if funds don't appear within 10 days.

### 3.2 Refund Amount

**Full Refund:**

- 100% of subscription fee paid
- Excludes transaction fees (cannot be refunded by us)
- Excludes taxes (must be claimed through tax authority)

**Example:**

```
Subscription Fee:      $99.00
Processing Fee (Stripe): $1.39  ← Not refunded
Total Charged:        $100.39

Refund Amount:         $99.00
```

### 3.3 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the **original payment method used** (not a different card).

- **Credit Card:** Appears as credit to original card
- **PayPal:** Refunded to PayPal account
- **Bank Transfer:** Refunded to original bank account

## 7. ANNUAL SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 7.1 Refund Eligibility

Annual subscriptions are handled differently:

- **First 30 days:** Full refund available
- **31-365 days:** Service credits only
- **No refund after 1 year**

### 7.2 Service Credits

If outside the 30-day window, you receive:

```
Months Remaining × Monthly Price = Credit

Example:
Annual subscription: $990/year ($82.50/month)
Refund requested: Day 60
Months remaining: 11
Service credits: 11 × $82.50 = $907.50
```

Service credits can be:

- Applied to future subscriptions
- Transferred to another user
- Requested as refund after 6 months

## 10. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q: What counts as "using features"?

**A:** Using a feature means:

- Accessing the feature
- Creating or modifying content
- Exporting or downloading data
- Running actions or reports

Merely viewing doesn't count (limited viewing allowed).

### Q: Can I get a refund for an annual subscription after month 2?

**A:** Annual subscriptions have a 30-day (not 14-day) refund window. After 30 days, you get service credits instead.

### Q: Do I lose access immediately after refund?

**A:** No. For monthly subscriptions, you keep access until the end of the current billing period.

### Q: What if Stripe charged me twice?

**A:** Contact us immediately at support@[yourdomain.com]. We'll issue a full refund for the duplicate charge right away.

### Q: Can I convert a refund to service credits?

**A:** Yes. When requesting a refund, you can opt for service credits instead, which cover future months at no charge.

### Q: What if I paid via a different payment method than my current card?

**A:** Refunds go to the original payment method. If that method is closed, refunds go to your registered bank account instead.

### Q: How do I check my refund status?

**A:** Log in to your account and go to Billing → Transactions. Click on the transaction to see refund status.
